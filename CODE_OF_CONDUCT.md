# Code of Conduct

**TL;DR — don't be a dick.**

If you need the longer version:

- Be kind to other contributors. Constructive criticism is welcome;
  personal attacks, harassment, and pile-ons are not.
- Assume good faith. Most rough edges in PR review are translation
  problems between people with different contexts, not malice.
- Don't share anyone's private information (contact details,
  location, real-name unmaskings) without their explicit consent.
- If a thread is generating more heat than light, the maintainer may
  close, lock, or hide it.
